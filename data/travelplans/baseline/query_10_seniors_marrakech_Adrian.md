## Assumptions and gaps
- This is a baseline plan using only Tavily web-search evidence, not live booking engines or supplier APIs.
- Currency conversion used for Moroccan dirham costs is approximate; verify current rates in 2026.
- July in Marrakech is very hot, so the itinerary deliberately avoids dense midday sightseeing.
- The plan assumes both travelers are comfortable with short walks on uneven Medina lanes; request taxis, luggage help and ground/lower-floor riad rooms if mobility is a concern.
- The most suitable base is a quiet traditional riad in or near the Medina, close enough for guided walks but not directly on the busiest square.
