# Baseline itinerary: 3 weeks in Cape Town on a €4,000 solo budget

## Goal and constraints understood
- Origin/destination: Munich → Cape Town → Munich.
- Dates: 1 June to 21 June 2026.
- Traveler: 1 adult, solo.
- Transport: flights required; local travel kept mostly to MyCiTi bus, train, walking, shared rides, and occasional Uber/Bolt.
- Accommodation: hostels only; social areas chosen to meet other travelers.
- Interests: hiking, Table Mountain, Cape Point, wildlife, surfing, local culture, budget travel, meeting people.
- Budget: €4,000 total including flights.
- Baseline limitation: this plan uses only Tavily web-search evidence, not live booking, fare, weather, safety, or availability tools.

## Search summary
- Return Munich–Cape Town economy flights in June 2026 appear roughly €750–€1,100 from aggregators/airline pages; exact 1–21 June fare must be verified before booking: [Google Flights result](https://www.google.com/travel/flights/flights-from-munich-to-cape-town.html), [Ethiopian Airlines route page](https://www.ethiopianairlines.com/en-de/flights-from-munich-to-cape-town).
- Social hostel base: [Long Street Backpackers](https://www.longstreetbackpackers.co.za/) describes itself as a communal city-centre hostel; hostel dorm rates from aggregators appeared roughly €15–€25/night, but require live confirmation.
- Local transport: [MyCiTi fare calculator](https://www.myciti.org.za/en/myconnect-fares/fare-calculator/) confirms distance/time-band fares and Mover packages; airport/city estimates from search were about R60–R100.
- Hiking safety: SANParks/Table Mountain guidance found via search recommends not hiking alone, hiking in groups, carrying a map, checking weather, and telling someone your plan.
- Table Mountain Cableway: operating times and prices vary by season/weather; verify on the official cableway site before using it: [Table Mountain Cableway visitor info](https://www.tablemountaincablecar.com/visitor-info).
- Cape Point/Boulders are in Table Mountain National Park; verify international visitor tariffs on [SANParks Table Mountain entry fees](https://www.sanparks.org/parks/table-mountain/rates-entry-fees).
- Surfing: Muizenberg surf schools show winter is viable and beginner-friendly; [Surf Emporium price list](https://surfemporium.co.za/price-list/) showed private lessons around R535 and a 4-lesson package around R1,605.
- Culture: free/tip-based walking tours cover Bo-Kaap and Cape Town history: [Free Walking Tours Cape Town](https://freewalkingtourscapetown.co.za/), [Walk Cape Town Bo-Kaap](https://walkcapetown.com/bo-kaap-walking-tour/).
- Robben Island tours should be booked through the museum; prices/times require date confirmation: [Robben Island Museum tour types](https://www.robben-island.org.za/tour-types-test/).
- Weekend food/culture market: [Oranjezicht City Farm Market](https://ozcf.co.za/ozcfmarket/) lists Saturday 08:00–14:30 and Wednesday night market closed June–July.
