## Budget summary
- Flights Cologne–Copenhagen return for 2: €350–€500 estimate
- Hotel SP34 or similar boutique/design hotel, 2 nights: €500–€700 estimate
- Local transport / City Pass / airport transfers: €60–€100 estimate for 2
- Bike rental: €20–€50 estimate for 2
- Museums:
  - Designmuseum Danmark: about €38 for 2 based on DKK 140 adult admission found via Tavily
  - DAC: €45–€60 estimate for 2; verify current ticket price
- Food and drinks:
  - Høst dinner: €115–€175 estimate for 2
  - Selma lunch: €70–€110 estimate for 2
  - Marv & Ben dinner: €180–€260 estimate for 2 before premium wine
  - Other breakfasts, coffees, lunch/drinks: €200–€320 estimate
- Estimated total: **€1,578–€2,313**
- Practical target to stay under €2,000:
  - Book flights early and aim for ≤€450 total.
  - Choose Hotel SP34 or similar around ≤€600 for 2 nights.
  - Keep one dinner wine-light, or choose Høst + Marv & Ben rather than ultra-luxury tasting menus such as Alchemist/Geranium.
  - Avoid large design purchases in the core trip budget.
