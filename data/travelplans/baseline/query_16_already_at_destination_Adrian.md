# Baseline itinerary: 3 days in Rome

## Goal and constraints understood
- Destination: Rome
- Dates treated as 3 free days: **Sunday July 5, Monday July 6, Tuesday July 7, 2026**; July 8 is treated as the endpoint / return to business schedule.
- Travelers: 1 adult
- No flights or hotel needed.
- Budget: **€400** for activities, food, local transport, and experiences.
- Interests: **Roman history, street food, Colosseum, Trastevere, hidden gems / off-tourist-trail Rome**.
- Baseline limitation: this plan uses **Tavily web-search evidence only**; booking availability, exact 2026 seasonal hours, and final ticket prices should be verified before purchase.
