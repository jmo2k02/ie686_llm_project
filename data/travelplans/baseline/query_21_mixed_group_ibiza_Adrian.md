## Budget summary
- Target total budget: **€12,000**.
- Estimated baseline spend:
  - Flights Dusseldorf–Ibiza return for 6 adults + 1 child + toddler, with bags/seats: **~€2,400–€3,200**.

  - Groceries / villa breakfasts / some villa dinners: **~€700–€1,000**.

  - Activities: aquarium, caves, water sports, possible Formentera ferry: **~€500–€900**.
  - Couple nightlife tickets/taxis/drinks for 2–3 nights: **~€500–€1,000**.
- Practical budget target: **~€11,800–€12,500** depending mainly on the villa and flights.
- To stay reliably under €12,000:
  - Cap villa at about **€6,500–€6,800** total.
  - Do 2–3 villa dinners instead of restaurants.
  - Limit Formentera if ferry prices are high.
  - Book flights early and avoid premium luggage bundles where possible.
