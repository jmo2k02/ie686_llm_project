# Baseline itinerary: accessible Munich to Barcelona trip, 5–10 September 2026

## Goal and constraints understood
- Travelers: 2 adults, one wheelchair user.
- Route and dates: Munich → Barcelona, 5 September 2026; Barcelona → Munich, 10 September 2026.
- Transport: flight, with wheelchair assistance and suitable seating requested.
- Accommodation: fully accessible hotel with lift access and roll-in shower.
- Interests: Gaudí architecture, accessible beaches, local food, accessible museums.
- Budget: €4,000 total.
- Accessibility standard used for this baseline: step-free or wheelchair-reachable plans wherever Tavily evidence was found; any item requiring direct confirmation is flagged.

## Budget summary
- Flights Munich–Barcelona return for 2: €500-€900 estimate.
- Accessible hotel, 5 nights, central Barcelona with lift and roll-in shower: €1,250-€1,900 estimate.
- Accessible taxis/local transport: €300-€550 estimate, depending on number of pre-booked adapted taxis.
- Attractions and museums: €200-€350 estimate for 2, depending on disability concessions and selected ticket types.
- Food: €850-€1,200 estimate for breakfasts, lunches, dinners, market snacks and drinks.
- Contingency/accessibility buffer: €300-€500 for taxi delays, equipment needs, room upgrade, or private accessible transfer.
- Estimated total: €3,400-€5,400.
- Budget fit: A careful version should fit the €4,000 budget if flights are booked early, hotel rate is near €250-€300/night, and most transfers are standard accessible taxis. If hotel rates exceed €350/night or adapted private transfers are needed daily, the plan may exceed budget.

## Unscheduled or uncertain items
- Exact flights, seat assignments, fares, wheelchair stowage approval, and aircraft type are not confirmed because this baseline only used Tavily search.
- The hotel is a candidate, not a booking. The exact accessible room must be guaranteed in writing with roll-in shower, lift access, door widths, bed height, toilet grab bars, and turning space.
- Sagrada Família official 2026 ticket prices/opening hours were not fully verified from an official source in Tavily results; schedule is based on likely daytime opening and secondary accessibility evidence.
- Restaurant accessibility is not fully verified for every meal. Each restaurant should be contacted directly for step-free entry, accessible toilet, table height, and indoor route.
- Assisted bathing at Nova Icària depends on the city’s 2026 beach-service calendar, weather and staffing. The City Council page indicates mid beach season includes 14–27 September, while this trip is 5–10 September; exact September 5–10 assisted-bathing hours must be checked closer to travel.
- Public transport elevator status can change daily; taxis are used as the conservative accessible default.

## Assumptions and gaps
- Assumption: the traveler can transfer to an aircraft seat with assistance, or will specify WCHC/WCHS/WCHR needs to the airline as appropriate.
- Assumption: a direct Munich–Barcelona flight will operate on both travel dates; this is likely but not confirmed.
- Assumption: a central Eixample/Plaça Catalunya hotel base is preferable because it reduces long transfers and puts Casa Batlló, La Pedrera, restaurants and transit close by.
- Assumption: accessible taxis are available when pre-booked; same-day availability may be limited.
- Gap: no live hotel booking engine, flight inventory, wheelchair-taxi dispatch, or official restaurant accessibility database was available to this baseline agent.
- Before booking, request written confirmations from airline, hotel, taxi provider, and key attractions so the trip is genuinely fully accessible rather than only “accessibility-likely.”
