- Driving-distance evidence: Tavily found Frankfurt–Dublin by road/ferry at about 1,330–1,360 km using distance-planning sources; actual driving time and cost require live routing.
- Accommodation evidence: [Abbey Court Hostel](https://www.abbey-court.com/) is a central budget hostel with 24h reception; [Generator Dublin / Hostelworld listing](https://www.hostelworld.com/hostels/p/48058/generator-dublin/) places Generator in Smithfield near Jameson Distillery and Temple Bar.

- Hiking/coast evidence: [Visit Dublin Howth Cliff Path Loop](https://www.visitdublin.com/howth-cliff-path-loop) describes a 7.8 km loop from Howth DART station taking about 2 hours; [Dublin Coastal Trail](https://www.visitdublin.com/guides/dublin-coastal-trail) is accessible by DART/Irish Rail.
- Wicklow hiking evidence: [Glendalough official site](https://glendalough.ie/) notes year-round access to the monastic site, lakes and walks; [Discover Ireland Glendalough Visitor Centre](https://www.discoverireland.ie/wicklow/glendalough-visitor-centre) highlights the lakes, monastic site and hiking.
- Pub evidence: [The Brazen Head](https://brazenhead.com/), [The Temple Bar Pub](https://thetemplebarpub.com/visit/), and [Visit Dublin: O’Donoghue’s Bar](https://www.visitdublin.com/o-donoghue-s-bar) support the pub/music choices; O’Donoghue’s lists live music seven nights a week in the Visit Dublin snippet.
- Tolls/parking evidence: [Transport Infrastructure Ireland](https://www.tii.ie/en/news/press-releases/toll-charge-increase-from-1st-january-2026/) gives 2026 M50 toll context; Tavily reported motor-car M50 toll around €3.80. Dublin city parking costs are variable and treated as estimates.
