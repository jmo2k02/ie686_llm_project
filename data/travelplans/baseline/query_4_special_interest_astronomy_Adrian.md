# Baseline itinerary: Hamburg to La Palma dark-sky astronomy trip, 12–19 Nov 2026

## Goal and constraints understood
- Solo traveller: 1 adult.
- Origin/destination: Hamburg, Germany → La Palma, Canary Islands, Spain.
- Dates: 12–19 November 2026, 7 nights.
- Transport: flight required; local mobility assumed by rental car because Roque de los Muchachos, Garafía and hiking trailheads are difficult by bus.
- Accommodation preference: rural house or small hotel in a dark-sky area near Roque de los Muchachos Observatory.
- Main interests: astronomy, stargazing, dark-sky reserve, observatory visit, hiking.
- Budget: €2,000 total.
- Baseline limitation: this plan uses Tavily web evidence only; no live flight, hotel, car-rental or tour-booking validation was available.

- Accommodation evidence points to rural stays in the northwest, especially Garafía/Puntagorda, including La Palma tourism’s northwest accommodation listings with examples from about €68/day: [La Palma northwest region](https://en.la-palma.travel/region/northwest/).
- Flight evidence suggests Hamburg–La Palma routes may be direct or one-stop, but November 2026 schedules/prices require verification closer to booking; reference found: [Skyscanner HAM–SPC](https://www.skyscanner.com/routes/ham/spc/hamburg-international-to-la-palma.html).
- Airport transfer/public-transport evidence: La Palma airport bus information found via travel sources, but for this itinerary a rental car is recommended because Garafía/Roque access is remote.
- Hiking evidence includes official tourism information for [Caldera de Taburiente](https://visitlapalma.es/en/hiking/la-palma/caldera-de-taburiente/) and trail references for Roque de los Muchachos ridge routes.
