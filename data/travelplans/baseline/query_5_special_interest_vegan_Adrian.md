# Baseline itinerary: Cologne to Amsterdam, 3–6 October 2026

## Goal and constraints understood
- Trip: 2 adults travelling from Cologne to Amsterdam, 3 nights / 4 days.
- Dates: 2026-10-03 to 2026-10-06.
- Budget: €2,000 total for two.
- Diet: strictly vegan; meals scheduled only at vegan / fully plant-based restaurants or plant-based market stops.
- Accommodation preference: eco-certified hotel or sustainable guesthouse.
- Interests: cycling, vegan restaurants, vegan/organic markets, sustainability, eco-friendly activities.
- Transport preference: train over flying; plan uses rail both ways.
- Baseline limitation: this itinerary uses Tavily web-search evidence only; live train fares, hotel availability, restaurant reservations, and 2026 event calendars must be rechecked before booking.
