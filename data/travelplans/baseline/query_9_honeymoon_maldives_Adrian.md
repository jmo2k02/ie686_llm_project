# Baseline itinerary: Honeymoon in the Maldives from Berlin, 10–20 Oct 2026

## Goal and constraints understood
- Origin: Berlin Brandenburg Airport, Germany.
- Destination: Maldives, using Velana International Airport, Malé/MLE.
- Dates: 10 Oct 2026 to 20 Oct 2026.
- Travelers: 2 adults, honeymoon.
- Budget: €8,000 total.
- Required transport: flights from Berlin.
- Accommodation requirement: luxury overwater bungalow or high-end beach resort.
- Interests: snorkelling, diving, spa treatments, romantic sunset dinners, relaxation, private beach.
- Baseline limitation: this plan uses Tavily web-search evidence only; flight fares, resort availability, room category pricing, and activity pricing must be rechecked before booking.

## Search summary
- Flight evidence: [Turkish Airlines Berlin–Maldives page](https://www.turkishairlines.com/en/book-flights-from-berlin-to-maldives) shows Berlin–Maldives round-trip fares from about €916 on sample dates; [Qatar Airways Berlin–Maldives page](https://www.qatarairways.com/en-us/destinations/flights-to-maldives/from-berlin.html) and [BER Airport Qatar Airways page](https://ber.berlin-airport.de/en/flying/airlines-ziele/airlines-uebersicht/qatar-airways-qr.html) support one-stop routing via Doha.
- Resort choice: [Adaaran Prestige Vadoo official site](https://www.adaaran.com/prestigevadoo/) — adults-only, premium all-inclusive, overwater villas with plunge pools, butler service, non-motorized watersports, and complimentary speedboat transfers when booked direct under stated offer conditions.
- Room fit: [Adaaran Sunset Water Villa with Private Pool and Jacuzzi](https://www.adaaran.com/prestigevadoo/villas/sunset-water-villas/) fits the overwater bungalow / honeymoon requirement.
- Transfers: [Adaaran transfers page](https://www.adaaran.com/prestigevadoo/facilities/transfers/) confirms resort speedboat transfer access from Malé; third-party snippets suggest 15–25 minutes, but exact cost/complimentary status must be confirmed with the booking.
- Experiences: [Adaaran excursions page](https://www.adaaran.com/prestigevadoo/experiences/excursions/) supports excursions; [Adaaran romantic getaway offer](https://www.adaaran.com/prestigevadoo/offers/storrington-collective-romantic-getaway/) mentions candlelit beach dinner for stays over 7 nights and a photoshoot, subject to offer terms.
- Tax: [Maldives Inland Revenue Authority green tax page](https://www.mira.gov.mv/Pages/View/whatisgreentax) confirms green tax applies to tourists; search evidence indicates resort green tax is about USD 12 per guest per night from 2025 onward, to be verified on invoice.

## Budget summary

  - Based on Tavily evidence from Turkish Airlines showing sample Berlin–Maldives fares from about €916 per person, with October 2026 fares needing live confirmation.
- Resort: 9 nights at Adaaran Prestige Vadoo, overwater villa / Sunset Water Villa style, premium all-inclusive: **€4,900 estimate**  
  - This is an assumed direct-booking/promotional target; official site advertises offers but exact Oct 2026 total was not verified.
- Speedboat transfers: **€0–€500 estimate**  
  - Plan assumes direct-booking complimentary transfer if eligible; otherwise budget may rise.
- Green tax: **about €200 estimate**  
  - Based on Maldives green tax evidence of about USD 12 per guest per night for resorts; confirm on invoice via [MIRA](https://www.mira.gov.mv/Pages/View/whatisgreentax).
- Paid extras: diving/snorkelling excursions, spa treatments, special dinner/photos: **€700 estimate**  
  - Can be reduced by choosing included non-motorized watersports and only one paid spa/diving day.
- Berlin airport transfers/miscellaneous: **€120 estimate**.
- **Estimated total: €8,020 if transfers are not complimentary; about €7,520–€7,800 if direct-booking complimentary transfers and romantic-dinner offer apply.**
- Budget recommendation: book only if the resort quote including taxes and transfers is **≤ €5,100** and flights are **≤ €2,200 total**; otherwise switch to a lower villa category for 2–3 nights or reduce paid diving/spa extras.
