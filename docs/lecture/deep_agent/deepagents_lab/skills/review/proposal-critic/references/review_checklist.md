# Review checklist

Check the draft against these questions:

## Coverage

- Are all four outline questions answered?
- Is the document still an outline proposal rather than a final report?
- Is the main body written as an executive summary in prose instead of as a bullet list?

## Specificity

- Are datasets, APIs, benchmarks, and models named explicitly?
- Is the data gathering plan concrete enough to execute?

## Workflow quality

- Is the multi-agent workflow step-based and plausible?
- Are the agent roles clearly different from each other?

## Evaluation quality

- Are success metrics measurable?
- Is there at least one baseline, benchmark, or comparison point?

## Evidence quality

- Are claims backed by the research notes and source URLs?
- Are assumptions clearly marked as assumptions?
- Is there a short references section that matches the sources actually used in the writing?
